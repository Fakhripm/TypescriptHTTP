Chirpy Recipe

This is a simple recipe for making Chirpy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of chirpy flakes
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • Fresh fruits (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix chirpy flakes and honey in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and stir well.
  3. Top with fresh fruits if desired.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
